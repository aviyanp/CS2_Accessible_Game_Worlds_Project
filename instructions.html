Honeydew Island

INSTRUCTIONS



Our main character is you- a farmer who wants to live a calm, relaxing life on his own farm island. Honeydew Island is a farming game, somewhat like Stardew Valley, where you slowly plant and harvest crops while expanding and upgrading your farm and island. The score called "Reputation" and once a certain reputation is met, the game ends. In the game, you will receive a few cheap seeds at the start of the game which you will plant. You wait till the seeds grow and you will sell it for gold coins. A trader will sometimes randomly come and you can buy more expensive seeds, or by reaching a certain reuptation, you will receive level up rewards. For playing with a keyboard, use WASD or the arrow keys for movement and use space or enter and action keys after being prompted and use "esc" to pause the game. With a controller, use the joystick to move around, A and X for action keys, and Y to pause. He will add vibration when the trader comes and other important events happen.
